    Some of my hardware projects:

    I love working with my hands and building things.

    • SAP-1 Computer Building an 8 bit computer from scratch on breadboards, was initially thinking of doing it at the transistor level but the amount of breadboards I would need was too damn high. Super fun project, got to learn about computer architecture and was able to see how the computer industry came about

    • Smartvents Wifi connected vents that open/close dynamically to maintain different temperature levels in all the rooms of a house. Initially made as a prototype for an Idea I was pitching. Ended up winning the competition, got funding to bring idea to life. A hardware iot project. Idea came about from personal problems - disputes with roommates on what the apartment temperature should be.

    • Mechatronics car for P.Eng Competition Got to work with 2 of my best mates. We built an amazing small race car. Won Judge's award. I designed the elecrical system from scratch, was the first time I had to really think and design a circuit from scratch.

    • FireFighting Robot Built a fire-truck robot that located fire in a maze and put it out.

    • FIRST Robotics & VEX Robotics 2012-2015 Built some amazing robots. My favourite seasons were Grade 10 VEX and Grade 11 VEX. Will add detail later.

    • Woodshop walking humanoid Created a robot that walks on 2 legs

    • Electro-mechnical Rube Goldbergs Rube Goldbergs are fun, been building them since before I knew they were called Rube Goldbergs. All my Rube Goldbergs have a high presence of lowlevel circuitry, not just mechnical energy transfers.
